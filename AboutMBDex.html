  • Welcome to MBDex
    Simply by placing trades,
    you can help your favourite projects!


    MBDex is MoonBlast's first utility to hit the market,
    with the primary intention of funding MoonBlast network projects.

    Here's how it works:

    - Visitors use our swap functionality to buy and sell their favourite coins
    - A 0.25 percent fee is deducted from each trade which is still less than PancakeSwaps' fee!
    - This fee is re-distributed to projects in the MoonBlast Network!

    MoonBlast Pre-ico begins in:

    Benefits for Investors

    - Investors support MoonBlast network projects, simply by trading any BSC token!
    - Access to a trustless swap, which protects users' funds, privacy, and limited personal data.
    - A clear oversight of funds transferred to projects, to ensure no private gain.



    Benefits for Partner Projects

    - Projects in partnership with MoonBlast have access to fee advertisement on the DEX.
    - Additional financial support to assist with project protocol, liquidity, or marketing costs.
    - Instant access to a greater variety of MoonBlast utility products that can be customised
    for project-specific applications.
