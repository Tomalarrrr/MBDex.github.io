  • Wallet & Card Utility
    Latest Status: Design Phase



    Our goal is to create a wallet interface that includes a debit card utility,
    so that users can spend cryptocurrency on everyday purchases.
    In order to attract new crypto investors who have yet to enter the market,
    we also want this utility to be user-friendly for all abilities.
    New wallet users will also be able to participate in our MoonBlast investor programme,
    which will educate them on the space before making significant investments.


    We gladly accept partnerships to bring this utility to life.
    If you believe your project or organisation is a good fit,
    please contact an admin on the MoonBlast Discord group.
